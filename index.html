ABC Compliant

Moon Daughters Mobile Imbibing provides professional bartending services for private events that comply with California's Department of Alcohol Beverage Control guidelines. To ensure a compliant event, the host must provide all alcohol that is to be served, and no selling or transactions for alcohol are allowed. Additionally, the event may not be accessible by the public. Refer to §23399.1 of the ABC Act for more information.
